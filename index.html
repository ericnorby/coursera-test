Pizzas

Detroit Style

Detroit Style Pizza is known for its airy crust, rectangular shape, and caramelized cheese that runs down the side of the pan. If you are a fan of pan pizzas, this is the one for you.

Neapolitan Style

The classic pizza is baked in a very hot oven and has a super thin crust, so this is the best pizza to highlight good quality ingredients. Don't overload your pie, though, or it'll fall apart!

New York Style

Chewy, thin crust and processed cheese. What could be better?
